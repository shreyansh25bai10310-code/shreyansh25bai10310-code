<h2>🍽️ EatAtEzz — Discover Hidden Food Gems</h2>
</summary>

### 📖 Overview

**EatAtEzz** is a startup focused on helping people discover authentic local eateries, hidden food stalls, and trending food destinations that are often overlooked by traditional food platforms.

Unlike conventional restaurant listing apps, EatAtEzz emphasizes community-driven recommendations, food playlists, and personalized discovery.

---

### 🎯 Problem Statement

Many exceptional local food vendors lack digital visibility because they are not listed on major food discovery platforms.

This makes it difficult for users to explore authentic local cuisine while reducing opportunities for small businesses.

---

### 💡 Solution

EatAtEzz enables users to:

- Discover hidden local food places
- Explore curated food playlists
- Follow food creators
- Save favorite restaurants
- Rate food experiences
- Share discoveries with friends
